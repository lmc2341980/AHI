# Introduction

This document describes the long-term vision of the AHI (Artificial Hybrid Intelligence) project. It defines the direction of building an intelligence ecosystem capable of preserving, inheriting, evolving, and transmitting knowledge across generations.

AHI is not just an artificial intelligence system.

AHI aims to build a planetary-scale knowledge infrastructure where individuals, families, organizations, nations, and communities can preserve their memories, experiences, skills, expertise, and heritage for future generations.
