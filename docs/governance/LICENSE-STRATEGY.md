# AHI License Strategy

## Purpose

This document defines the licensing strategy for the AHI (Artificial Hybrid Intelligence) ecosystem.

The objective is to balance:

* Open collaboration
* Ecosystem growth
* Knowledge sharing
* Intellectual property protection
* Long-term sustainability
* Commercial viability

## Public Repositories

Public repositories serve as:

* Open standards
* Ecosystem documentation
* Community collaboration
* Research sharing
* Developer onboarding
* International cooperation

Public repositories do not automatically grant access to proprietary technologies contained within private repositories.

---

## Private Repositories

Private repositories contain:

* Proprietary technologies
* Patentable inventions
* Commercial know-how
* Core algorithms
* Strategic research
* Confidential implementation details

Access may be granted through licensing, partnerships, investment agreements or authorized collaboration programs.

---

## Intellectual Property Principles

AHI respects:

* Individual ownership
* Family ownership
* Organizational ownership
* Community ownership
* National sovereignty
* Intellectual property rights

Participation in the AHI ecosystem does not automatically transfer ownership of knowledge, memories or intellectual assets.

---

## Long-Term Objective

The AHI ecosystem aims to create a sustainable balance between:

* Open innovation
* Knowledge preservation
* Contributor incentives
* Commercial sustainability
* Human-centered governance
* Long-term civilization continuity
