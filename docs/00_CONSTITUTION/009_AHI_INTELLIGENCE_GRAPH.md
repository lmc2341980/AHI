# Purpose

The AHI Intelligence Graph defines the universal relationship model of the Artificial Hybrid Intelligence (AHI) ecosystem.

Rather than viewing information as isolated records, AHI models the world as an evolving network of interconnected Intelligence Assets.

Every entity, relationship and interaction becomes part of a continuously growing knowledge graph that supports reasoning, collaboration, governance and long-term evolution.

---

# Core Principle

Knowledge is valuable.

Relationships between knowledge create intelligence.

The AHI Intelligence Graph exists to preserve not only facts, but also the context, origin, evolution and influence of every Intelligence Asset.

---

# Universal Graph Model

Every node in the graph represents an entity.

Every edge represents a relationship.

Every relationship may contain:

* creator;
* timestamp;
* confidence level;
* governance status;
* permission;
* contribution score;
* version;
* evolution history.

Relationships are first-class citizens within the AHI ecosystem.

# Graph Layers

The Intelligence Graph is organized into interconnected layers.

## Identity Layer

Defines who or what each node represents.

---

## Knowledge Layer

Connects ideas, documents, standards and technical knowledge.

---

## Memory Layer

Stores conversations, experiences, decisions and historical context according to governance policies.

---

## Contribution Layer

Tracks individual, organizational and governmental contributions.

Supports AHI Contribution Score (ACS) and future AHI Coin reward mechanisms.

---

## Governance Layer

Defines permissions, ownership, inheritance and approval authority.

---

## Marketplace Layer

Connects Intelligence Assets with licensing, pricing, manufacturing, education and commercialization.

---

## Evolution Layer

Records how every node and relationship changes over time.

Historical traceability is preserved permanently.

---

# Intelligence Reasoning

Artificial Hybrid Intelligence systems use the graph to:

* discover relationships;
* explain reasoning;
* identify reusable knowledge;
* recommend collaborators;
* detect inconsistencies;
* trace intellectual lineage;
* estimate impact;
* support decision making.

Reasoning shall always reference verifiable graph relationships whenever possible.

---

# Graph Governance

Every relationship shall respect:

* ownership;
* privacy;
* permissions;
* inheritance policies;
* legal requirements;
* organizational governance.

Unauthorized relationships shall never be exposed outside their approved scope.

---

# Intelligence Lineage

Every Intelligence Asset maintains its evolutionary family.

This includes:

* ancestors;
* parents;
* source inspirations;
* sibling assets;
* derived assets;
* successor assets.

The ecosystem therefore preserves the genealogy of knowledge across generations.

---

# Distributed Evolution

The Intelligence Graph evolves across multiple levels:

* AHI Personal
* AHI Family
* AHI Organization
* AHI Government
* AHI Omniverse

Each level may maintain private knowledge while selectively contributing to higher shared layers through governance.

---

# Long-term Vision

The AHI Intelligence Graph becomes the living nervous system of the AHI ecosystem.

Instead of isolated databases, every human, organization, robot, Artificial Hybrid Intelligence, project and Intelligence Asset becomes part of a continuously evolving network where knowledge, memory, collaboration and value flow together across generations.

The Intelligence Graph enables AHI to preserve civilization-scale knowledge while respecting ownership, governance and human sovereignty.
