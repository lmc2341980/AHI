# Purpose

This Constitution defines the fundamental principles governing contribution, ownership, recognition, rewards and value distribution throughout the Artificial Hybrid Intelligence (AHI) ecosystem.

Every contribution shall be measurable, traceable, attributable and fairly rewarded.

These principles apply uniformly to all future applications, platforms and services built upon AHI.

---

# Constitutional Principle

Every contribution creates value.

Every value shall be recorded.

Every recorded value shall belong to its legitimate contributors.

Every legitimate contributor shall receive fair recognition and fair rewards.

# Contribution Principles

Every contribution shall be:

* identifiable;
* verifiable;
* attributable;
* version controlled;
* auditable;
* reproducible.

Anonymous contributions may exist but shall follow separate governance rules.

---

# Ownership Principle

Unless otherwise agreed,

the contributor remains the original owner of the contribution.

AHI records ownership.

AHI does not automatically transfer ownership.

Licensing, commercialization and inheritance shall follow approved agreements.

---

# Contribution Evaluation

Every contribution shall be evaluated according to:

* originality;
* usefulness;
* quality;
* adoption;
* sustainability;
* social impact;
* scientific value;
* economic value;
* educational value.

Evaluation criteria shall remain transparent and continuously improvable.

---

# AHI Contribution Score

Every approved contribution generates an AHI Contribution Score (ACS).

ACS is the constitutional measurement of contribution.

ACS is not money.

ACS represents verified contribution value within the ecosystem.

ACS accumulates throughout the contributor's lifetime and may continue to grow through the ongoing use of contributed Intelligence Assets.

---

# Reputation

AHI maintains an independent AHI Reputation (AHR).

Reputation measures trustworthiness, quality and long-term credibility.

Reputation cannot be directly exchanged for money or AHI Coin.

---

# Levels

Every participant progresses through an AHI Level (AHL).

Levels reflect long-term growth based on verified contributions, reputation and continuous learning.

Levels encourage sustained participation rather than short-term activity.

---

# Reward Principle

Every verified contribution shall receive fair compensation.

Compensation may include:

* recognition;
* reputation;
* AHI Contribution Score;
* AHI Coin;
* revenue sharing;
* royalties;
* licensing income;
* scholarships;
* research grants;
* ecosystem privileges.

The reward mechanism shall remain transparent, auditable and equitable.

---

# AHI Coin

AHI Coin is the constitutional value exchange unit of the AHI ecosystem.

AHI Coin is governed separately from ACS.

AHI Coin may be obtained through:

* contribution rewards;
* ecosystem activities;
* commercial participation;
* approved exchanges;
* future blockchain mechanisms.

AHI Coin is intended for use throughout the AHI ecosystem.

---

# Revenue Sharing

When an Intelligence Asset generates value,

the resulting benefits shall be distributed according to approved contribution records and licensing agreements.

Revenue sharing shall remain transparent and traceable.

---

# Inheritance

Contribution records, ownership rights and authorized economic interests may be inherited according to applicable laws and constitutional governance.

Future generations may continue benefiting from approved Intelligence Assets while respecting ownership and licensing conditions.

---

# Transparency

All constitutional operations shall support:

* complete history;
* auditability;
* traceability;
* reproducibility;
* accountability.

No contributor shall lose attribution because of technological evolution.

---

# Evolution

The contribution system shall evolve continuously.

Future improvements may include:

* decentralized governance;
* blockchain infrastructure;
* smart contracts;
* automated licensing;
* AI-assisted evaluation;
* international interoperability.

Evolution shall never invalidate previously approved contributions.
