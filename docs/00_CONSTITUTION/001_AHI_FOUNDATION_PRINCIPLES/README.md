# Purpose

This directory contains the official Foundation Principles of the Artificial Hybrid Intelligence (AHI) Constitution.

The Constitution defines the highest governing rules of the AHI ecosystem.

The Foundation Principles explain each constitutional principle in detail and provide the common interpretation for all repositories, products, Artificial Intelligence systems, robots, organizations and future implementations.

Every repository in the AHI ecosystem shall inherit these principles.

---

# Relationship with the Constitution


The Constitution defines **what AHI is**.

The Foundation Principles define **how the Constitution shall be interpreted**.

Architecture defines **how systems are organized**.

Standards define **how systems are implemented**.

# Principle Structure Standard

Every Foundation Principle shall use the same structure.

1. Name
2. Purpose
3. Definition
4. Why
5. Scope
6. Rules
7. Artificial Intelligence Interpretation
8. Examples
9. Related Standards
10. Related Repositories
11. Future Evolution
12. Version History

This structure guarantees consistency across the entire AHI ecosystem.

# Multilingual Principle

The Foundation Principles are maintained from a single authoritative source.

Official translations shall be generated and maintained through the AHI Linguistics framework.

Current official languages are:

* Vietnamese
* English
* Japanese
* Russian
* Hindi
* Arabic

Additional languages may be added in future versions.

---

# Governance

Changes to any Foundation Principle shall follow the Constitution Change Process.

No Foundation Principle may conflict with the AHI Constitution.

When conflicts exist, the Constitution always has the highest authority.

---

# Evolution

The Foundation Principles are living knowledge.

They may evolve over time while preserving compatibility with the Constitution and maintaining traceable version history.

Every change shall be reviewed, documented and approved according to the governance process.
