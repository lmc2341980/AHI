# Purpose

This document defines the standard lifecycle for every Intelligence Asset (IA) within the Artificial Hybrid Intelligence (AHI) ecosystem.

An Intelligence Asset is any intellectual creation, knowledge, memory, digital object or physical implementation that has value and can evolve over time.

The lifecycle ensures that every Intelligence Asset can be created, verified, governed, shared, commercialized, inherited and continuously improved.

# Lifecycle Stages

## 1. Creation

An Intelligence Asset is proposed.

---

## 2. Collaboration

Experts, organizations and Artificial Intelligence systems contribute improvements.

---

## 3. Verification

Technical, legal and quality reviews are performed.

---

## 4. Governance

Ownership, permissions, licensing and compliance are confirmed.

---

## 5. Publication

The Intelligence Asset becomes available according to its selected sharing model.

Examples:

* Private
* Family
* Organization
* Government
* Public
* Commercial
* Open Source

---

## 6. Contribution Evaluation

The ecosystem evaluates:

* originality;
* usefulness;
* impact;
* adoption;
* sustainability.

Results contribute to the AHI Contribution Score.

---

## 7. Commercialization

An Intelligence Asset may be:

* sold;
* licensed;
* rented;
* customized;
* manufactured;
* integrated into products or services.

---

## 8. Continuous Evolution

Every approved improvement creates a new version while preserving historical traceability.

---

## 9. Inheritance

According to governance rules, an Intelligence Asset may be transferred to:

* individuals;
* families;
* organizations;
* governments;
* future generations.

---

## 10. Preservation

Approved Intelligence Assets become part of the permanent knowledge heritage of the AHI ecosystem.

# Human Authority

The creator remains the owner of the Intelligence Asset unless ownership is explicitly transferred according to governance rules and applicable law.

Artificial Intelligence assists throughout the lifecycle but does not assume ownership.

---

# Long-term Vision

Every Intelligence Asset created within AHI becomes a reusable building block for future innovation.

Instead of being forgotten after its initial use, each asset may continue evolving, generating value and contributing to future generations through collaboration, governance and continuous learning.
