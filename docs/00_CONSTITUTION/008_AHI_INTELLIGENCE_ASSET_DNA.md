# Purpose

This document defines the canonical DNA model for every Intelligence Asset (IA) within the Artificial Hybrid Intelligence (AHI) ecosystem.

The DNA model provides a unified identity, governance, ownership, lifecycle and evolution structure that enables every Intelligence Asset to be created, managed, shared, commercialized and inherited consistently across the ecosystem.

---

# Principle

Every Intelligence Asset has a unique identity.

Every Intelligence Asset has a complete history.

Every Intelligence Asset can evolve.

Every Intelligence Asset remains traceable throughout its lifecycle.

---

# DNA Structure

Each Intelligence Asset shall contain the following core metadata.

## 1. Identity

* Asset ID
* Global Unique Identifier (GUID)
* Name
* Description
* Category
* Type
* Keywords

---

## 2. Ownership

* Original Creator
* Current Owner
* Co-owner(s)
* Custodian
* Beneficiary
* Inheritance Policy

---

## 3. Contributors

Each contribution shall record:

* Contributor
* Role
* Contribution Type
* Contribution Score
* Timestamp

---

## 4. Version Information

* Current Version
* Previous Version
* Parent Asset
* Child Assets
* Branch
* Merge History

---

## 5. Lifecycle Status

Examples:

* Draft
* Reviewing
* Approved
* Published
* Commercial
* Archived
* Deprecated
* Inherited

---

## 6. Governance

* Approval Status
* Reviewer
* Compliance Status
* Applicable Policies
* Audit Trail

---

## 7. Licensing

* Copyright
* Patent
* Trademark
* Trade Secret
* Open Source License
* Commercial License
* Custom License

---

## 8. Marketplace Information

* Price
* Rental Option
* Subscription
* Availability
* Marketplace Category

---

## 9. Contribution Economy

* AHI Contribution Score (ACS)
* Contribution Level
* Reward History
* Revenue Sharing
* Reputation

---

## 10. AHI Coin Integration

When enabled:

* AHI Coin Value
* Reward Transactions
* Marketplace Transactions
* Staking Eligibility
* Treasury History

The implementation of AHI Coin shall be governed by a dedicated blockchain project and related governance documents.

---

## 11. Relationship Graph

Each Intelligence Asset may reference:

* Related Assets
* Dependencies
* Derived Assets
* Supporting Evidence
* External References

This relationship graph enables the ecosystem to build a continuously evolving knowledge network.

---

## 12. Knowledge Classification

Each Intelligence Asset shall be classified according to:

* Personal
* Family
* Organization
* Government
* Public

and may additionally be classified by:

* Security Level
* Domain
* Industry
* Technology Area
* Language

---

## 13. Evolution History

Every approved change shall record:

* Previous State
* New State
* Reason
* Decision Reference
* Reviewer
* Approval Date
* Lessons Learned

---

## 14. Digital Legacy

Where applicable:

* Legacy Owner
* Successor Policy
* Preservation Requirements
* Long-term Accessibility

---

## 15. Interoperability

The DNA model shall remain compatible across all AHI repositories, applications, APIs, cloud services, robotics platforms and future technologies.

---

# Design Principles

The DNA model shall be:

* Unique
* Verifiable
* Traceable
* Evolvable
* Interoperable
* Extensible
* Human Governed

---

# Long-term Vision

The AHI Intelligence Asset DNA establishes a common metadata language for the entire AHI ecosystem.

Every idea, memory, algorithm, document, robot, AI model, design, course, dataset and future digital creation can be managed using the same foundational structure.

This shared DNA enables collaboration, inheritance, commercialization, governance and continuous evolution across generations while preserving ownership, transparency and historical integrity.
