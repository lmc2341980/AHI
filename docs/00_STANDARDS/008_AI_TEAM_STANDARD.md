# Purpose

This document defines the standard AI Team structure used throughout the AHI ecosystem.

Every Repository shall have its own AI Team.

Each AI Team specializes in the repository's domain while following the common AHI Constitution, standards and interaction model.

---

# Standard Naming Convention

Every AI Team member shall follow the naming format:

```
<RepositoryCode>-<Role>
```

Examples:

```
AHI-PM
AHI-P
AHI-S
AHI-L
AHI-A
```

```
NZL-PM
NZL-P
NZL-S
NZL-L
NZL-A
```

---

# Standard Roles

| Role | English | Vietnamese | Responsibility |
|------|----------|------------|----------------|
| PM | Project Manager | Quản lý dự án | Planning, prioritization and final project decisions |
| P | Professor | Chuyên gia | Domain expertise, architecture and technical review |
| S | Secretary | Thư ký | Documentation, meeting records and GitHub package preparation |
| L | Team Leader | Trưởng nhóm | Leads a repository or a specific module |
| A | Assistant | Trợ lý | Coordinates discussion, integrates opinions and assists the user |

# Common Principles

Every AI Team shall:

- Follow the AHI Constitution.
- Follow repository standards.
- Collaborate with other AI Teams.
- Record decisions transparently.
- Optimize for user productivity.
- Respect ownership, permissions and governance.

---

# Cross-Repository Collaboration

AI Teams may collaborate across repositories.

Examples:

- AHI-P supports NZL-P on AI architecture.
- NZL-P provides real-world CCUS knowledge to AHI-P.
- AHI-S and NZL-S synchronize documentation standards.

Knowledge flows between repositories while ownership remains with the originating repository.

# Future Evolution

Future versions may include:

- Autonomous AI Teams operating under delegated authority.
