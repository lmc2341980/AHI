# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Hybrid Intelligence evolves from an individual intelligence into a planetary intelligence ecosystem.

It is the master evolution architecture of AHI.

Every repository shall support one or more evolution dimensions defined herein.

---

# Five Evolution Dimensions

## 1. Cognitive Evolution

The evolution of understanding.

```
Know
    ↓
Understand
    ↓
Understanding
    ↓
Sự biết
```

**"Sự biết"** is a foundational concept of AHI-Lang.

It represents the ability to integrate knowledge, memory, experience and values into new intelligence.

# Evolution Principle

Every AHI evolves in parallel along multiple dimensions.

Progress in one dimension does not automatically imply progress in another.

Each AHI Personal, Organization and Government maintains its own evolutionary path while contributing to the collective evolution of the AHI ecosystem.

---

# Founder Note

The AHI Evolution Framework reflects the long-term vision of building a trustworthy planetary intelligence ecosystem where human knowledge, expert thinking, memory and civilization continue to evolve across generations while preserving ownership, governance and consent.
