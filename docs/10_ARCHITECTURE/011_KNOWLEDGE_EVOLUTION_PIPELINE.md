# Three-Level Knowledge Evolution Model

AHI defines three levels of human understanding.

## Level 1 — Knowing

Vietnamese:

Biết

Definition:

Receiving facts.

Example:

"I know that water boils at about 100°C."

Characteristics:

- Memory
- Observation
- Data collection

---

## Level 2 — Understanding

Vietnamese:

Hiểu biết

Definition:

Knowing why something happens and how it works.

Example:

"I understand why water boils."

Characteristics:

- Relationships
- Cause and effect
- Analysis
- Explanation

---

## Level 3 — Awareness

Vietnamese:

Sự biết

Definition:

A deep integrated awareness that combines experience, intuition, ethics, values and continuous learning.

Awareness enables better decisions rather than only correct answers.

Characteristics:

- Experience
- Wisdom
- Ethics
- Context
- Adaptation
- Long-term thinking

AHI considers Awareness to be the highest stage currently defined.
