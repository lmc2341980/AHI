# Purpose

This document defines how information evolves inside the AHI ecosystem.

Every piece of knowledge has a lifecycle from creation to standardization.

---

# Information Layers

```
Ideas

↓

Drafts

↓

Knowledge

↓

Guides

↓

Machine Rules

↓

Automation

↓

Products

↓

Marketplace
```

---

# Layer Definitions

## Ideas

Initial thoughts.

May be incomplete.

---

## Drafts

Raw conversations.

Meeting notes.

Brainstorming.

Original AI chats.

No editing.

These are the short-term memory of AHI.

---

## Knowledge

Validated knowledge.

Stored in:

docs/

---

## Guides

Human operational manuals.

Stored in:

help/

---

## Machine Rules

Instructions for AI.

Stored in:

ai/

---

## Automation

Programs that verify rules automatically.

Stored in:

ai/validators/

---

## Products

Software.

Services.

Hardware.

Expert Systems.

---

## Marketplace

Commercial Intelligence Assets.

---

# Draft Repository

The folder:

drafts/

stores every original conversation.

No summarization.

No modification.

Future AI systems may analyze drafts to improve AHI continuously.

---

# Source of Truth

GitHub is the long-term memory.

Chats are temporary memory.

Only approved documents become standards.

---

# Human Approval

Every important architectural decision must be approved by a human before becoming an official AHI standard.

---

# Long-term Vision

AHI continuously transforms human experience into reusable Intelligence Assets through a structured lifecycle while preserving ownership, traceability and governance.
